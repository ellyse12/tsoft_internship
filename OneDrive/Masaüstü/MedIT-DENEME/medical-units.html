Medical Units

Explore the various medical units at MedIT, equipped with advanced technology and expert professionals to provide top-notch care.

Cardiology

Our cardiology unit offers specialized care for heart-related conditions, including diagnostics, treatments, and surgery.

Neurology

Our neurology unit focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of brain, spine, and nervous system disorders.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic unit specializes in musculoskeletal care, offering treatments for bones, joints, and soft tissues.

Pediatrics

The pediatrics unit provides comprehensive medical care for children, including vaccinations, general check-ups, and treatment for illnesses.

Dermatology

Our dermatology unit offers skin care treatments, including diagnosis and therapy for various skin conditions.

Gynecology

The gynecology unit specializes in women's health, offering services such as preventive care, treatments, and surgeries.
